Mapo Tofu (麻婆豆腐)

Ingredients

  • 1 block soft tofu, cubed
  • 150g ground pork or beef
  • 1 tbsp doubanjiang (chili bean paste)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp chili oil
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorn powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup water or broth
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and cook the ground pork until browned.
  2. Add doubanjiang and garlic, stirring until fragrant.
  3. Pour in water or broth and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add tofu cubes gently and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Season with soy sauce, sugar, and chili oil.
  6. Stir in the cornstarch slurry to thicken the sauce.
  7. Sprinkle Sichuan peppercorn powder and green onion on top.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
