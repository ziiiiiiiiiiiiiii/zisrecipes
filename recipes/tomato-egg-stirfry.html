Tomato & Egg Stir-Fry (番茄炒蛋)

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with 1/4 tsp salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs until just set. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften and release juices.
  4. Season with sugar and remaining salt. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and ketchup.
  5. Return eggs to the pan and gently fold together.
  6. Cook 30–60 seconds until saucy.
  7. Top with green onion and serve with rice.
