Steamed Pork with Mushrooms (香菇蒸肉饼)

Ingredients

  • 300g ground pork
  • 3–4 dried shiitake mushrooms, soaked and minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2–3 tbsp water or mushroom soaking liquid
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Instructions

  1. Mix pork with soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, pepper, sesame oil, and cornstarch.
  2. Add water gradually and stir until sticky.
  3. Fold in mushrooms and green onion.
  4. Spread mixture into a shallow dish.
  5. Steam over medium heat for 12–15 minutes.
  6. Serve with rice and spoon the juices over top.
