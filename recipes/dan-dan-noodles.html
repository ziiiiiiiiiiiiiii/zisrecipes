Dan Dan Noodles (担担面)

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the ground pork and cook until browned and slightly crispy.
  3. Add soy sauce and a pinch of sugar to the pork. Stir and cook for another minute, then remove from heat.
  4. In a serving bowl, whisk together the chili oil, sesame paste (or peanut butter), minced garlic, black vinegar, sugar, and Sichuan peppercorn powder until smooth.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the bowl and toss thoroughly so they absorb the sauce.
  6. Top the noodles with the seasoned pork.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions.
  8. Serve immediately while hot and spicy.
