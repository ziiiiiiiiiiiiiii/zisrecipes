Steamed Fish (清蒸鱼)

Ingredients

  • 1 whole fish (sea bass, tilapia, or snapper)
  • 2–3 slices ginger
  • 2 stalks green onion
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hot oil
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • Fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Place ginger and half the green onion inside the fish.
  2. Set fish on a plate and drizzle with Shaoxing wine.
  3. Steam over high heat for 8–10 minutes.
  4. Remove ginger/onion used for steaming.
  5. Top with fresh green onion and cilantro.
  6. Heat oil until shimmering and pour over aromatics.
  7. Drizzle soy sauce around the fish and serve.
